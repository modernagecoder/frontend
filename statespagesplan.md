## SEO STRATEGY PER PAGE

### Meta Title Formula
```
Best Online Coding Classes in [CITY] | Kids, Teens & Adults | Modern Age Coders
```
- Max 60 characters
- City name first (most important keyword)
- Age groups as modifiers
- Brand name last

**City-specific examples:**
- Jaipur: `Best Online Coding Classes in Jaipur | Kids, Teens & Adults | Modern Age Coders`
- Kochi: `Best Online Coding Classes in Kochi | Kids & Teens | Modern Age Coders`
- Chandigarh: `Best Online Coding Classes in Chandigarh | CBSE & Python | Modern Age Coders`

### Meta Description Formula (155-160 chars)
```
Looking for coding classes in [CITY]? Modern Age Coders offers live online coding & maths for kids, teens and adults in [CITY]. Python, AI, Web Dev. Rated 4.9/5. Book a free demo!
```

**Always include:**
- "coding classes in [CITY]" or "near me in [CITY]"
- "live online" (USP differentiator)
- At least 2 course names (Python, AI, Web Dev, Scratch)
- "4.9/5" rating social proof
- CTA: "Book a free demo"

## PER-CITY CONTENT GUIDE

### How to make EACH page genuinely unique (SEO + conversion)

Every page MUST have these 5 unique elements. Without them Google treats it as a doorway page.

1. **City-specific intro paragraph** — 3–4 sentences about that city's tech/education ecosystem
2. **Local neighborhood coverage list** — 15–25 areas the city is known for
3. **City-specific FAQ** — at least 3 FAQs unique to that city (school boards, languages, local concerns)
4. **Local social proof angle** — reference the city's schools, boards, or IT ecosystem
5. **Local schema** — correct GPS coordinates, correct city/state in areaServed
