Reduced Motion Test

To test reduced motion:

  1. On Windows: Go to Settings > Ease of Access > Display > Show animations in Windows
  2. On Mac: Go to System Preferences > Accessibility > Display > Reduce motion
  3. On browsers: You can also use DevTools to emulate prefers-reduced-motion

When reduced motion is enabled, all animations and transitions below should be disabled or minimized.
