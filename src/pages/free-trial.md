Warm-up read (level, confidence, spark) → a real lesson (coding: build and run something; maths: a concept from meaning, solved aloud) → one notch past comfortable (how the child responds to challenge is the most useful signal) → the honest placement read.

## The parent's checklist (evaluate any online class with this)

Who talks more? Are questions asked before answers shown? Does the level adjust live? How are mistakes treated? Is the child lighter afterward? Was the read honest rather than flattering?
