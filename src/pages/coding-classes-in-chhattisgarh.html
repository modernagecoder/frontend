Why It Matters Here

Why Coding Matters for Students in Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is one of India's fastest-developing states, with a strong industrial backbone in Bhilai and Korba and a growing capital region around Raipur. With IIT Bhilai and NIT Raipur anchoring a serious engineering pipeline, and Naya Raipur emerging as a planned smart city, demand for future skills like coding is rising fast among Chhattisgarh families.

What's often missing is access to quality, hands-on teachers — especially in smaller towns and tribal districts. Modern Age Coders bridges that gap with live, small-batch online classes where a real instructor knows each student's pace, reviews their projects and keeps them building, wherever they live.

Aligned with Chhattisgarh's school boards

Our curriculum complements the Chhattisgarh Board (CGBSE), CBSE and ICSE. We strengthen the logical thinking and maths fundamentals that help across every subject, and our advanced maths track supports JEE and the CG PET. Explore Python & AI, Java and DSA & full-stack tracks built for this.

Learn in the language you're comfortable with

Classes are taught in English, and instructors can explain concepts in Hindi when it helps a student understand faster. We focus on genuine understanding, not rote learning — and we run a dedicated Coding for Girls programme to bring more girls across Chhattisgarh into tech.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best online coding classes in Chhattisgarh?+

Modern Age Coders offers live online coding classes in Chhattisgarh for kids aged 6–12, teens aged 13–18 and adults. Courses include Scratch, Python, Web Development, Java, AI/ML and Data Structures & Algorithms. Classes run in small batches of 5–8 students with expert instructors, and students across Raipur, Bhilai, Bilaspur and Durg can join. Every new learner gets a free demo class.

Do you offer coding classes for CGBSE, CBSE and ICSE students?+

Yes. Our coding and maths curriculum supports students from the Chhattisgarh Board (CGBSE), CBSE and ICSE. Lessons complement school work and strengthen logical thinking and problem solving alongside the school syllabus.

How much do coding classes cost in Chhattisgarh?+

Group coding classes start at ₹1,499 per month for 2 classes a week. A Mini Batch of 3–4 students is ₹2,499 per month, and personalised 1-on-1 classes are ₹4,999 per month. A free demo class is available before you enrol.

Are classes available in Hindi as well as English?+

Yes. Our instructors teach in English and can explain concepts in Hindi when a student is more comfortable, so children across Chhattisgarh can learn coding in the language that suits them best.

Which cities in Chhattisgarh do you serve?+

Because classes are live and online, we serve every district of Chhattisgarh. We have a dedicated city page for Raipur, and we also teach students in Bhilai, Bilaspur, Durg, Korba, Rajnandgaon, Raigarh and beyond.

Do you teach Python and AI to kids and teens in Chhattisgarh?+

Yes. Kids begin with Scratch and move into Python from around age 10, and teens learn Python, Java, Web Development and AI/Machine Learning by building real projects. See our dedicated Python & AI and AI & ML for Teens tracks.

Can your maths classes help with JEE and CG PET preparation?+

Yes. With IIT Bhilai and NIT Raipur in the state, Chhattisgarh has a strong engineering pipeline, and our advanced maths programme builds the fundamentals needed for JEE and the CG PET. We focus on concept clarity, problem-solving speed and reasoning rather than rote learning.

Do you offer coding classes specially for girls in Chhattisgarh?+

Yes. We run a dedicated Coding for Girls track and supportive batches that encourage girls in Chhattisgarh to build games, websites and AI projects with confidence.

Are the classes live or recorded?+

All classes are live and interactive with a real instructor, so students can ask questions in real time. Each session is also recorded and shared, so learners in Chhattisgarh can revise lessons anytime.
