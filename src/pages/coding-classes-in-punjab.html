Why It Matters Here

Why Coding Matters for Students in Punjab

Punjab combines a proud, hard-working culture with a strong appetite for opportunity — and increasingly that opportunity is in technology. Mohali sits beside the Chandigarh IT hub, Ludhiana is one of India's biggest industrial cities now modernising fast, and institutions like IIT Ropar, Thapar University in Patiala and NIT Jalandhar anchor a serious engineering pipeline. For Punjabi families — many with strong global connections — coding is a skill that opens doors at home and abroad.

What's often missing is quality, personal teaching that reaches every town, not just the big cities. Modern Age Coders bridges that gap with live, small-batch online classes where a real instructor knows each student's pace, reviews their projects and keeps them motivated — from Amritsar to the smallest district.

Aligned with Punjab's school boards

Our curriculum complements the Punjab School Education Board (PSEB), CBSE and ICSE. We strengthen the logical thinking and maths fundamentals that help across every subject, and our advanced maths track supports JEE and engineering-entrance preparation. Explore Python & AI, Java and DSA & full-stack tracks built for this.

Learn in the language you're comfortable with

Classes are taught in English, and instructors can explain concepts in Punjabi or Hindi when it helps a student understand faster. We focus on genuine understanding, not rote learning — and we also run a dedicated Coding for Girls programme to encourage more girls across Punjab into tech.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best online coding classes in Punjab?+

Modern Age Coders offers live online coding classes in Punjab for kids aged 6–12, teens aged 13–18 and adults. Courses include Scratch, Python, Web Development, Java, AI/ML and Data Structures & Algorithms. Classes run in small batches of 5–8 students with expert instructors, and students across Amritsar, Ludhiana, Jalandhar, Mohali and Patiala can join. Every new learner gets a free demo class.

Do you offer coding classes for PSEB, CBSE and ICSE students?+

Yes. Our coding and maths curriculum supports students from the Punjab School Education Board (PSEB), CBSE and ICSE. Lessons complement school work and strengthen logical thinking and problem solving alongside the school syllabus.

How much do coding classes cost in Punjab?+

Group coding classes start at ₹1,499 per month for 2 classes a week. A Mini Batch of 3–4 students is ₹2,499 per month, and personalised 1-on-1 classes are ₹4,999 per month. A free demo class is available before you enrol.

Are classes available in Punjabi as well as English?+

Yes. Our instructors teach in English and can explain concepts in Punjabi or Hindi when a student is more comfortable, so children across Punjab can learn coding in the language that suits them best.

Which cities in Punjab do you serve?+

Because classes are live and online, we serve every district of Punjab. We have a dedicated city page for Amritsar, and we also teach students in Ludhiana, Jalandhar, Mohali, Patiala, Bathinda, Pathankot, Hoshiarpur and beyond.

Do you teach Python and AI to kids and teens in Punjab?+

Yes. Kids begin with Scratch and move into Python from around age 10, and teens learn Python, Java, Web Development and AI/Machine Learning by building real projects. See our dedicated Python & AI and AI & ML for Teens tracks.

Can your maths classes help with JEE preparation?+

Yes. Our advanced maths programme builds the strong fundamentals needed for JEE and other engineering entrances. We focus on concept clarity, problem-solving speed and reasoning rather than rote learning.

Do you offer coding classes specially for girls in Punjab?+

Yes. We run a dedicated Coding for Girls track and supportive batches that encourage girls in Punjab to build games, websites and AI projects with confidence.

Are the classes live or recorded?+

All classes are live and interactive with a real instructor, so students can ask questions in real time. Each session is also recorded and shared, so learners in Punjab can revise lessons anytime.
