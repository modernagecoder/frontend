Which One is Right for You?

Not sure which plan fits best? Here's a simple guide to help you decide.

Choose Group Classes

₹1499/month

Group classes work great when you enjoy learning with others and can keep up with a steady pace.

  • You enjoy learning with peers and healthy competition
  • You're comfortable asking questions in a group
  • You can follow a fixed class schedule
  • You learn well at a moderate pace
  • You want collaborative project experience
  • Budget-friendly option works for you

Choose Personalized 1-on-1

₹2499/month

Personalized classes are ideal when you need individual attention or have specific learning needs.

  • You feel shy or hesitant asking questions in groups
  • You need more time to understand concepts
  • You have a busy schedule and need flexibility
  • You want curriculum tailored to your goals
  • You prefer undivided teacher attention
  • You're a slow learner who needs extra patience
