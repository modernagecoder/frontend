Let's be straight: a certificate by itself doesn't impress anyone who matters. Recruiters and college admissions have seen thousands of "course certificates." What actually moves the needle is proof you can do the work — and that's exactly what we pair with the certificate.

This is a Modern Age Coders course-completion certificate, not a government or vendor exam credential (like the C++ Institute's). We'd rather be honest than oversell. What makes it genuinely valuable is the portfolio of working programs behind it — programs you designed, built and can explain line by line.

Built on real depth

The certificate sits on top of real learning — OOP, the STL and Data Structures & Algorithms — not a weekend crash course. Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official or government-recognised C++ certification?+

We are honest about this: it is a Modern Age Coders course-completion certificate, not a government or vendor (e.g. C++ Institute) credential. What gives it real weight is that it is backed by a portfolio of working programs you built and can explain — demonstrated skill, which is what colleges and interviewers actually value.

What does the certificate certify?+

It certifies that you completed the C++ course and the projects in it — Core C++, OOP, the STL, Data Structures & Algorithms and a capstone project. It comes with your project portfolio, so it is proof of what you can build, not just attendance.

Will the certificate and portfolio help my resume or college?+

Yes. The real value is the portfolio of programs you built and can walk through in an interview or college submission, with the certificate as a summary. Recruiters and colleges care far more about demonstrated, explainable projects than a certificate alone — and you get both.

What do I need to do to get certified?+

Complete the C++ course modules — Core C++, OOP, the STL and DSA — and build the projects, finishing with a capstone you design. On completion you receive your course-completion certificate and your portfolio. Pace is flexible based on your plan.

How much does the C++ certification course cost?+

Group classes start at ₹1,499 per month for 2 classes a week. A Mini Batch of 3–4 students is ₹2,499 per month, and 1-on-1 mentoring is ₹4,999 per month. The certificate is included on completion, and a free demo class is available first.

Is it suitable for both school and college students?+

Yes. The course and certificate suit teens building early skills and a portfolio, as well as college students who want a structured C++ credential and projects to support placements. We place each learner at the right level after a free demo.
