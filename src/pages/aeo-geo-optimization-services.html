Questions

Frequently asked

What is AEO and GEO?

AEO is Answer Engine Optimization, making your content the direct answer that engines like Google AI Overviews and voice assistants read out. GEO is Generative Engine Optimization, making your brand a source that generative tools like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity cite in their answers.

How is this different from SEO?

SEO wins ranked links on a results page. AEO and GEO win the answer itself, so your brand is named even when the user never clicks. They share technical foundations, but the content, structure and authority signals are tuned for machine reading and citation.

Can you guarantee my brand gets cited by ChatGPT?

No honest provider can guarantee a specific AI citation, because the engines are closed and constantly changing. We build the structured content, schema and authority signals that make citation far more likely, and we track where your brand appears over time.

How do you measure results?

We track branded and unbranded mentions across AI answers, presence in AI Overviews for target questions, referral traffic from AI tools, and the growth of your answer-ready content and schema coverage.

Do we still need SEO?

Yes. AEO and GEO build on solid SEO foundations. We usually run them together so you win both the ranked links and the AI answers.
