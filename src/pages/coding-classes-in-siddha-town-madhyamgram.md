How we teach

## Fundamentals first. Tools second. Understanding always.

Every class is led live by an instructor who can see your child's screen and work. Here is what that looks like, week to week — four steps that repeat.

01

### A real teacher, live

No pre-recorded playlists. Your child writes code while a teacher watches, asks questions and corrects mistakes as they happen — good tuition, without the travel.

02

### Build by hand first

Students build a thing themselves before they are allowed the built-in shortcut. The shortcut is a reward for understanding what it really does, never a replacement for it.

03

### Maths meets code

Loops meet multiplication; functions meet algebra; coordinates meet graphs. We connect the school maths a Madhyamgram child studies to the code they write in class.

04

### Progress you can see

Parents get visibility into what was taught and what comes next. Every session is recorded, so a missed Saturday or a tricky topic can be revisited before the next class.

The AI Trap

### We show students where AI fails — on purpose.

AI tools can write code, but they cannot understand for your child. In our classes we set up deliberate moments where an AI answer looks right and is quietly wrong, and the student has to catch it. Children who only copy from a chatbot get stuck the moment the problem is unfamiliar. Children who understand the fundamentals can use AI as a tool and still know when it is wrong. That judgement is the skill we are actually building.
