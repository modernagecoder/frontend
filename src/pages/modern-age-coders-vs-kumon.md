# Modern Age Coders vs Kumon

**In one paragraph:** Pick Kumon if your child needs a daily drill habit and thrives on repetition-to-mastery with minimal adult instruction. Pick Modern Age Coders if your child needs someone to actually teach: a live mentor who explains concepts, answers questions in the moment, and applies maths to real projects and code.

## Where Kumon genuinely wins

- Building a daily discipline habit through the worksheet-a-day structure.
- Pure calculation fluency through repetition, refined since 1958.
- Very young starters (from age 3; our live classes start at 6).
- Children who self-motivate through progressive levels.

## Where Modern Age Coders wins

- Someone actually teaches: live mentors explain, spot misunderstandings, fix them the same hour.
- Understanding over speed, applied in projects and code.
- One all-inclusive price vs per-subject fees plus registration.
- Coding and maths in one school.
- Two Kumon subjects ($300 to $400/month) can cost more than our 1-on-1 maths AND a coding course combined.

## The per-class maths

MAC 1-on-1 maths: $150 across 8 live classes = $18.75 per taught hour. Kumon is $150 to $220 per subject for worksheets, not classes.

## FAQ

**Is Kumon a class or worksheets?** Worksheets by design, with brief check-ins. Modern Age Coders is the opposite: every session is a live taught class.

**Which is better for a child behind in maths?** Fluency gap: Kumon repetition works. Understanding gap: live teaching finds and fixes the misunderstanding.

**Can we try before paying?** Yes, a free live demo class, no card required.
