## Why Bokaro families start here

From City Centre (Sector 4) and Sector 1 to Sector 2 and Sector 3, families across Bokaro are choosing live online coding classes that go beyond a certificate. We build for real skills — the kind that hold up in an interview or a first internship.

Bokaro is a steel town by design. The Government of India decided in the early 1960s to build a modern public-sector steel plant here in collaboration with the Soviet Union, and Prime Minister Indira Gandhi laid the foundation stone of the Bokaro Steel Plant in 1966. The district itself is young, carved out in 1991 from parts of Dhanbad and Giridih. That planned-township character still shapes the city. Schools follow the Jharkhand board, the Jharkhand Academic Council, and engineering seats in the state are filled on JEE Main rank through state counselling rather than a separate state exam.

Whether your child is in a JAC, CBSE, ICSE or IGCSE school, the plan is the same: start at the right level, build something real, and keep the momentum going.
