## Live online, taught to a private-tutor standard

Every Modern Age Coders class is delivered 100% live online by a full-time engineer or maths graduate, in a small batch where your child is seen, heard and corrected in real time. There are no pre-recorded videos, no rotating part-time tutors and no passive watching — only a live teacher, a shared screen and a project being built together. For Southern Avenue's pro-rigour families, this is premium, accountable, high-quality education without the daily drive across Gariahat.

### Live, never recorded

Every session is taught live by a real instructor who can see your child's code as it is written. Mistakes are caught and explained the moment they happen, the way a good private tutor on Sarat Bose Road would — except your child never leaves home. Nothing here is a recording you watch alone.

### Taught by full-time engineers

Classes are led by full-time software engineers and mathematics graduates, not college students filling a slot between exams. They have shipped real software and solved real maths, so a curious Southern Avenue child asking "but why does this work?" gets a proper answer, not a deflection.

### Genuinely small batches

Group batches are capped at 6-8, mini batches at 3-4, and 1-on-1 is always available. In a small live room every child speaks, codes and is questioned each session — the same individual attention these families expect from the best tutors on Dover Lane and Hindustan Park.

### Written 24-30 week curriculum

Each course follows a documented 24-30 week module map you can read in full before enrolling — exactly what intellectually serious families here ask for first. You see what your child will build by week six, week twelve and week twenty-four, and progress is tracked module by module.

### Project-based, not theory-dumped

Children build real, working things — a Rabindra Sarobar bird log, a Lake Market budget tool, a Dover Lane raga clock — not just pass tests. Project-based learning turns abstract syntax into something a child can demo to a grandparent, which is how the concepts actually stick.

### No commute across Gariahat

The lakeside stretch from Golpark to Lansdowne chokes at Gariahat, Rashbehari and Lake Market every evening. Live online erases roughly 60-90 minutes of signal-to-signal traffic per class day; your child finishes school, walks home along the Sarobar, and simply logs in.
