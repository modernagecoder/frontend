# Custom Software Development

Off-the-shelf tools force your business to bend to their limits. We build software that fits your process, connects to your existing systems, and grows with you.

## What we build

- **SaaS platforms**: multi-user products with accounts, billing, roles and dashboards.
- **Internal tools**: admin panels and operations tools that replace fragile spreadsheets.
- **Customer portals**: secure logins for orders, bookings, tickets, documents and accounts.
- **Dashboards and reporting**: live data views that turn raw numbers into decisions.
- **Integrations and APIs**: connect CRM, ERP, payments, accounting and third party tools.
- **Workflow automation**: turn repetitive manual steps into reliable automated pipelines.
- **Booking and marketplace systems**: scheduling, inventory, listings and transactions.
- **CRM and ERP modules**: custom modules where generic products fall short.

## How we work

Discover, then scope and quote in writing, then design with wireframes and a clickable flow, then build in short weekly iterations, then test and launch with data migration and monitoring, then optional ongoing support.

## Technology

Frontend: React, Next.js, Vue, TypeScript, Tailwind. Backend: Node.js, Python, Django, Express, Java. Data: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase, Redis. Cloud and ops: AWS, Vercel, Docker, CI/CD, monitoring. If your stack is different, we work in yours.

## Who it is for

Operations-heavy SMEs drowning in spreadsheets, service businesses that need bookings and invoicing in one tool, product companies building a reliable SaaS, and large organisations needing internal modules and integrations that off-the-shelf enterprise software cannot cover.
