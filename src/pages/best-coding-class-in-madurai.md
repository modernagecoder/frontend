From Anna Nagar and KK Nagar to Goripalayam and Tallakulam, families across Madurai are choosing live online coding classes that go beyond a certificate. We build for real skills — the kind that hold up in an interview or a first internship.

Madurai grew up around the Meenakshi Sundareswarar temple, the twin shrine of Meenakshi and Sundareswarar that still anchors the old city. Its education has depth too: Thiagarajar College of Engineering was established in 1957 by the philanthropist Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar, and Madurai Kamaraj University was set up by the state in 1965. The city also keeps the Gandhi Memorial Museum in the historic Tamukkam Palace. Schools follow the Tamil Nadu state board, with a regional examinations office in Madurai, and engineering admission is TNEA, the state's single-window counselling.

Whether your child is in a Tamil Nadu State Board, CBSE, ICSE or IGCSE school, the plan is the same: start at the right level, build something real, and keep the momentum going.
