# Algebra 2 tutoring online

**The 20-second answer:** a good Algebra 2 tutor organizes the course around function families (linear, quadratic, polynomial, exponential, logarithmic, rational, trigonometric) so fifteen units become one repeating story; rebuilds the Algebra 1 machinery the course silently assumes; and connects every topic to the SAT, which draws its hardest math from exactly this course.

## Why Algebra 2 breaks students

Geometry can be survived on intuition and Algebra 1 on procedure; Algebra 2 audits the account. Rented understanding gets repossessed: pattern-matched factoring collapses at rational expressions, un-understood exponents make logarithms hieroglyphics. The reframe: the course is one story told seven times, meet a family, learn its shape, solve its equations, transform it, apply it.

## The complete unit map

Functions and transformations; quadratics completed (factoring, completing the square with the actual square, formula, discriminant); polynomial functions (end behavior, zeros, remainder/factor theorems); radicals and rational exponents; exponential growth and decay; logarithms as exponent questions with translated laws; rational expressions on a factoring foundation; systems including 3-variable; sequences and series; unit-circle trigonometry; probability and statistics. Every row is SAT hunting ground.

## Signature teaching moves

Logarithms taught as riddles (2 to what power gives 8?) before notation; completing the square drawn as an actual square missing its corner; transformations with reasons, not memorized tables; real SAT questions inside each unit.
