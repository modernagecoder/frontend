# Online maths tutoring for Australia: from NAPLAN to Methods, taught properly

**The 20-second answer:** teach to the Australian Curriculum and your state's senior syllabus, treat NAPLAN bands as a symptom list (not a goal), and get the Year 11 Methods vs General choice right by destination first, readiness second, scaling a distant third.

## The road, mapped

F-6: fraction sense and fluency foundations. Years 7-10 (+ NAPLAN 7/9): the algebra handover, where gaps compound; calculator dependence masks weak arithmetic. Year 11 fork: NSW Standard/Advanced/Ext 1/Ext 2; VIC and QLD General/Methods/Specialist. Years 11-12: functions, calculus, statistics + state-exact exam craft. Verified against current ACARA and state-authority documentation.
