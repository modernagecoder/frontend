# Parent FAQ

## Getting started
Enrollment: free trial (one real hour + honest placement read) → pick course + two weekly slots → monthly billing, no registration fee, no contract, start same week. Ages 6-65, zero experience is the normal case. Coding or maths first? Whichever the child needs more; mentors teach both.

## Classes and method
Full 60-minute classes, 8/month, 2/week, all recorded (recordings expire — download promptly). Interactive = the child talks, builds and explains more than the teacher. Same mentor month after month; mismatches re-matched on request. Group (small, capped) vs 1-on-1: trial mentor recommends straight.

## Pricing and billing
Coding $40/mo group, $100/mo 1-on-1. Maths (premium track) $100 group, $150 1-on-1 — deliberately premium, still undercutting US tutoring norms of $200-$425/mo. No registration/materials fees or contracts. International payment: USD by card. Pause/cancel before the next billing month; policies linked in footer.

## Safety and tech
Live vetted mentors, end-to-end recordings, parents welcome any time, no public chat rooms; game-platform classes in private class environments, never public servers. Equipment: computer + browser + internet + mic. Missed classes: recordings + reschedules.

## Progress
Signals: recordings, shipped projects (/student-labs), school marks, explain-backs. Homework = small purposeful quests. Certificates + a real portfolio. Struggling maths: homework comfort in 4-6 weeks, durable understanding over a term — no grades-by-date promises. Advanced kids get genuine stretch (competition maths, USACO).

## Scheduling
Every time zone (US, UK/EU, Gulf, India, SG, AU/NZ); fixed weekly slots that move only when you ask; exam/vacation flexing. Fast human: WhatsApp +91 9123366161.
