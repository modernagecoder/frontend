## Where each one genuinely wins

Outschool built something real. Here is the fair split.

### Where Outschool is a better fit

- **Sampling and discovery.** One-off workshops let a child try Minecraft modding on Tuesday and space science on Friday, no commitment.
- **Unusual niche topics.** The catalog covers subjects no single school could: from Dungeons and Dragons maths to marine biology.
- **Pay-per-class flexibility.** No subscription; you buy exactly the classes you want.
- **US-based teachers by default.** If a same-culture, same-timezone teacher for casual classes matters most.

### Where Modern Age Coders wins

- **Deep skill needs a spine.** A curriculum where hour 30 builds on hour 29 produces ability no collection of one-offs can.
- **One mentor who knows your child.** The same teacher tracks progress, spots gaps and raises the bar at the right moments.
- **Interactive hours students wait for.** Full 60-minute live classes, twice a week, application-first, never filler.
- **Real shipped projects.** Our students publish live apps, games and AI tools with engineering feedback (see Student Labs).
- **Coherent cost for sustained learning.** A month of structured teaching costs what a handful of marketplace hours does.

## Frequently asked questions

Is Outschool good for learning to code?

For a first spark, absolutely: a fun one-off Scratch or Minecraft workshop can light the fire. The struggle comes after, because real coding ability is cumulative. Assembling a coherent path from separate marketplace listings, each with a different teacher who does not know what came before, is the parent's job on Outschool. At a school like ours, that is our job: one mentor, one curriculum, every hour building on the last.

How do prices actually compare?

Outschool classes typically run $10 to $30 per class hour as of July 2026, with multi-week series and 1-on-1 lessons often $50 to $100 or more; each teacher sets their own price. Modern Age Coders is $40 a month for 8 live small-group hours or $100 a month for 8 dedicated 1-on-1 hours. For sustained weekly learning, a month with us costs about what a few marketplace hours do, and every hour compounds.

Does teacher quality vary on Outschool?

By design, yes: it is an open marketplace and each teacher is independent, so families read reviews listing by listing. Our mentors are vetted and trained into one standard, and our whole school carries a single public record: 4.9 across 547 Google reviews, where parents name their mentors personally.

Can Outschool teach coding and maths together?

Only if you assemble it yourself from separate teachers who never talk to each other. We teach both as one integrated programme, so the maths shows up exactly when the code needs it, which is often the moment both subjects click.

Can we try Modern Age Coders before paying?

Yes. Every student starts with a free live demo class with a real mentor, no card details required, and billing is monthly with no lock-in. Details on our [zero-risk page](/guarantee).
