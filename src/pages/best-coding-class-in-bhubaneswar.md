## Why Bhubaneswar families start here

From Saheed Nagar and Nayapalli to Patia and Chandrasekharpur, families across Bhubaneswar are choosing live online coding classes that go beyond a certificate. We build for real skills — the kind that hold up in an interview or a first internship.

Bhubaneswar is one of India's first planned cities. The German architect Otto Konigsberger drew up its modern plan in 1946, and the city administration still lists it, alongside Chandigarh and Jamshedpur, among the country's earliest planned cities. It was inaugurated as the capital of Odisha two years later, on 13 April 1948. The engineering names families reach for sit outside the city core: IIT Bhubaneswar's permanent campus is at Argul, about 30 km out, and NISER is at Jatni. Inside the city is OUTR, the state technical university, formed in 2021 from the old College of Engineering and Technology. The route onward is JEE Main, with the OJEE committee running the counselling.

Whether your child is in a BSE Odisha, CBSE, ICSE or IGCSE school, the plan is the same: start at the right level, build something real, and keep the momentum going.
