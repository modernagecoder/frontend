Why Erode families start here

From Brough Road / GH Road and Perundurai Road to Sathy Road and Surampatti, families across Erode are choosing live online coding classes that go beyond a certificate. We build for real skills — the kind that hold up in an interview or a first internship.

Erode is the turmeric city of India. Its district administration carries the title, and Erode turmeric holds a Geographical Indication tag, traded through one of the country's biggest turmeric markets alongside a long textile and handloom industry. The city was also the birthplace of the social reformer Periyar, born here in 1879. Its engineering anchor is Kongu Engineering College at Perundurai, established in 1984. Schools follow the Tamil Nadu state board, and engineering admission is TNEA, the state's single-window counselling on Class 12 marks.

Whether your child is in a Tamil Nadu State Board, CBSE, ICSE or IGCSE school, the plan is the same: start at the right level, build something real, and keep the momentum going.

Coding classes in Erode — FAQs

Which is the best coding class in Erode for kids?
Modern Age Coders runs Erode's top-rated live online coding classes for kids aged 6-12. Children start with Scratch (games, animation, logic) and progress to Python. Batches are small (5-8 students), project-based, and taught by industry instructors. We serve every part of the city — Brough Road, Perundurai Road, Sathy Road, Veerappanchatram, Surampatti, Karungalpalayam, Thindal and GH Road — and out to Perundurai across the Kongu belt.
Do you offer online coding classes across Erode?
Yes. All classes are live and online, so a child on Brough Road, near the Perundurai Road colleges, at Sathy Road, Surampatti, Karungalpalayam, Thindal or out towards Chithode and Perundurai joins the same expert teacher from home — no traffic on GH Road or the Sathy Road junction. Sessions are interactive with live coding, screen sharing and recordings.
How much do coding classes cost in Erode?
Group classes (5-8 students) start at Rs.1499/month for 2 live classes per week. Mini-Batch (3-4 students) is Rs.2499/month and personalised 1-on-1 mentoring is Rs.4999/month. Every new student gets a free demo class first, and there are no hidden fees.
Will coding help my child with JEE, NEET and the board exams in Erode?
Yes. Erode families prepare hard for TNEA, JEE and NEET. Coding strengthens the exact logical reasoning, decomposition and problem-solving that competitive exams reward, and it directly supports CBSE and Tamil Nadu State board Computer Science plus the new NEP-2020 AI modules. Our Python and Java tracks line up with what is taught at Vellalar, CMS, St John's and Sengunthar schools.
What age is best to start coding in Erode?
Age 8 is ideal to begin with visual block-based coding (Scratch). For text-based languages like Python and Java, age 12-13 onwards works best. We design separate tracks for kids (6-12), teens (13-18) and college/working learners (18+).
What programming languages and courses do you teach in Erode?
Kids (6-12): Scratch, web design (HTML/CSS) and intro Python. Teens (13-18): Python, Java, Full-Stack Web Development (React/Node) and AI/ML. College (18+): Data Structures & Algorithms, MERN full-stack and AI/Data Science. We also teach school and Olympiad mathematics alongside coding.
Do you prepare college students in Erode for placements?
Yes. Our college track is placement-focused: 200+ DSA problems, mock interviews, MERN full-stack projects and AI/Data-Science portfolios. It is built for students from Kongu Engineering College (Perundurai), Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, Erode Arts & Science College and the region's other campuses targeting service and product IT roles in Coimbatore, Bangalore and beyond.
Do you provide certificates and is there a free demo?
Every student receives a completion certificate that strengthens school portfolios and college applications. Yes — we offer a free demo class before you enrol, and a full refund within the first paid week if it is not the right fit.
Do you teach coding to Tamil Nadu board students in Erode?
Yes. Erode schools run the Tamil Nadu state board, along with CBSE and ICSE, and our classes fit around any of them. We teach the programming itself, Python or Java, so the school computer paper stops being a memory test.
Can Kongu Engineering College students join your programming courses?
Yes. Kongu Engineering College at Perundurai has taught since 1984, so the fundamentals are well covered. The college track adds built work: real Python, Java, full-stack or AI/ML projects, carried to something that runs and can be opened in an interview.
