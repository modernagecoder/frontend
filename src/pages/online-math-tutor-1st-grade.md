# Online math tutor for 1st grade: from counting everything to knowing things

**The map:** add/sub within 20 with strategies (count on, make ten, doubles) + fluency within 10 · word problems as imagined stories, not keyword hunts · place value to 120 as real bundles · the equals sign as "same value as" (the two-week investment that pays off in algebra) · measurement, time to the half hour, shapes and halves.

## The signature moment

Make-ten discovered on ten-frames: 9+5 becomes 10+4 by sliding ONE counter, and the child finds it themselves. Strategy → game → mental move → known fact with understanding underneath: fluency built this way survives grade 2; crammed fluency famously does not.

## Honest answers

Fingers at six are the right tool; only counting-ALL persisting is the concern, and shaming fingers teaches hiding. Homework hatred at six = missing tools, and it reverses in 4-6 weeks. Year-end checklist: facts within 10 known, two strategies owned, bundles understood, stories imagined, equals sign read right.
