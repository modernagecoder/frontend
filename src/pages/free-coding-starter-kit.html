About the kit.

Is it really free? What do you get out of it?

Really free, and the trade is stated plainly on the form: we get to introduce ourselves once, usually a single WhatsApp about the free trial class. The kit is useful whether or not you ever take a class with us, deliberately so, because that is what makes it worth your email.

Will I get spammed?

No drip machine exists on our end to spam you with: your details reach a human coordinator. Expect at most one friendly follow-up. Our 547 Google reviews are from families, not from email-blast survivors.

My child is a teen or I am an adult beginner. Still useful?

Partially: the age map, tools list and judging checklist apply at every age, while the projects lean young. Teens and adults may prefer going straight to the coding roadmap page or booking the free trial for a placement read.

Do I need to buy anything to use the kit?

Nothing: every tool inside is free (Scratch, Python, MakeCode, VS Code), and the projects run on any ordinary computer. If a coding program tries to sell you software before teaching, the kit explains why that is a signal.

The download button appears right on this page after the form, no inbox digging needed. If anything misbehaves, WhatsApp us at +91 9123366161 and a human sends it directly.
