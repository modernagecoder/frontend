A Birla Legacy Since 1959 — Now Meets the AI Era

Founded in 1959 by industrialist Late B.K. Birla and managed by the Birla Vidya Vihar Trust, Mahadevi Birla World Academy stands at 17A Darga Road, Park Circus — an institution that has shaped minds for over six decades. With CBSE affiliation (ID: 2430003), MBWA has always prided itself on holistic development: from its composite sports complex Mahadevi Birla Krida Kunj with indoor swimming and skating, to its vibrant clubs like Symphony, Aalap, and Expressions. But in 2026, academic excellence alone is no longer enough. Students who graduate without coding fluency and advanced mathematical reasoning will find themselves outpaced by peers from schools where technology is woven into the curriculum from Class 1. Our coding and maths mentorship doesn't replace what MBWA already does brilliantly — it amplifies it, giving students from the Park Circus and Beniapukur neighbourhood the 21st-century skills that top universities and employers now consider non-negotiable.

The CBSE Academic Pressure Your Child Faces Daily

MBWA follows the CBSE board — arguably the most rigorous national curriculum in India. Students face periodical assessments, term exams, and the high-stakes Class 10 and 12 board examinations. Computer Science (Python-based from Class 11) and Information Technology are now core subjects, not optional extras. Yet most coaching centres near Park Circus, Sealdah, or Moulali teach a generic CBSE syllabus to 30-50 students from a dozen different schools. The result? Your child memorizes syntax but cannot write a single working programme from scratch. Our approach is fundamentally different: we track MBWA's exact chapter pacing, sync our teaching schedule with your child's periodical exam dates, and build projects that directly correspond to CBSE practical exam requirements. When Board exam day arrives, your child doesn't just know Python — they think in Python.

Coding + CBSE Maths: The JEE/WBJEE Multiplier Effect

MBWA consistently produces top engineering and medical aspirants. If your child dreams of IIT, NIT, BITS, or Jadavpur University, they need more than rote formula practice. When a student learns to write sorting algorithms in Python, they are simultaneously strengthening the mathematical reasoning that JEE and WBJEE demand. Our combined coding + maths curriculum creates a compound effect: students approach calculus, coordinate geometry, and probability with the structured thinking of a programmer. Parents near Ballygunge, Bhowanipore, and Entally consistently report that their children's marks improve not just in Computer Science but across Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics when they learn both coding and maths with us.

Flexible Batches Built for the MBWA Student Lifestyle

We know that MBWA students have packed days — school until 3 PM, then sports at Krida Kunj, club activities like Funky Fingers or FootLoose, and sometimes Interact Club community service. That is exactly why we offer evening batches (4 PM – 8 PM) on weekdays and full-day options on weekends. Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents of MBWA students want to know.

Are you officially affiliated with Mahadevi Birla World Academy? +
No. We are a completely independent educational provider. We design our batches to follow the exact CBSE syllabus, textbook pacing, and examination pattern that MBWA uses, giving our enrolled students a hyper-specific academic advantage. However, we hold no administrative, partnership, or endorsement ties with the school.
How do your classes fit around the MBWA school schedule? +
We offer weekday evening batches from 4 PM to 8 PM and weekend batches on Saturday & Sunday from 9 AM to 6 PM. These are specifically designed to fit around MBWA's school hours, sports at Krida Kunj, club activities, and private tuitions. Our live online format means zero commute time from your home near Park Circus, Darga Road, or anywhere in Kolkata.
What if my child misses a class? +
Every session is recorded in HD. The student can review it anytime from their dashboard. For 1-on-1 mentorship plans, classes are simply rescheduled to a convenient time — ensuring zero knowledge loss even during exam weeks or annual day preparations.

Absolutely. Many MBWA parents prefer their children to handle both logical fronts. Select “Both Subjects” in the form below and we will architect a schedule where neither class overlaps with school, sports, or other activities.
Who are the mentors? +
Our Computer Science mentors are full-stack developers with industry experience. Mathematics faculty features specialists who have navigated Olympiads, JEE, and top-tier admissions (IITs/NITs) themselves. We do not hire generic tutors — every mentor is vetted for both subject mastery and the ability to teach.
Do you offer vacation coding camps? +
Yes! We run intensive Summer Coding Camps (April-May) and Puja Holiday Bootcamps (September-October). MBWA students can complete an entire module in 4-6 weeks. Check our Summer Camp page for upcoming dates.
Do you provide certificates? +
Yes. Every student receives a verified completion certificate. These are valuable additions to school portfolios, college applications, and demonstrate coding proficiency to universities worldwide.

Fill the form below. An academic advisor will call you within 24 hours to understand your child's exact needs, run a diagnostic assessment, and recommend the right batch. The demo class is completely free with no obligation.
