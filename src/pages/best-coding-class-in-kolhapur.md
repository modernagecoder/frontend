## Why Kolhapur families start here

From Rajarampuri and Shahupuri to Tarabai Park and Laxmipuri, families across Kolhapur are choosing live online coding classes that go beyond a certificate. We build for real skills — the kind that hold up in an interview or a first internship.

Kolhapur carries the Chhatrapati legacy and its own crafts. The Mahalaxmi, or Ambabai, temple here is one of Maharashtra's four Shakti Peethas, and the New Palace, built in the 1880s, is still the residence of the Chhatrapati of Kolhapur. The city's name is a mark of quality: both the Kolhapuri Chappal, the handcrafted leather sandal, and Kolhapur Jaggery carry a Geographical Indication tag from the Government of India. Shivaji University has served the region since 1962. Schools follow the Maharashtra state board through its Kolhapur divisional office, and engineering admission is MHT-CET through the state's process.

Whether your child is in a Maharashtra SSC/HSC, CBSE, ICSE or IGCSE school, the plan is the same: start at the right level, build something real, and keep the momentum going.
