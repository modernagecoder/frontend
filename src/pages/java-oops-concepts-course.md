## The 4 pillars of OOP
1. **Encapsulation** — bundle data + methods, hide internals (private fields, getters/setters)
2. **Inheritance** — reuse and extend classes ("is-a"), `class Car extends Vehicle`
3. **Polymorphism** — one name, many forms (overloading + overriding)
4. **Abstraction** — expose essentials, hide complexity (abstract classes, interfaces)
