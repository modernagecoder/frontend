Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior coding experience?

Zero experience needed. We start from absolute basics. The curriculum is designed for complete beginners who want to reach a professional level as efficiently as possible.

I work full-time. Can I still keep up?

Absolutely. We schedule evening and weekend batches specifically for working professionals. Sessions are recorded, so you never fall behind. Expect 1–2 hours of practice between sessions.

I'm outside India — can I join?

Yes! We have learners from 30+ countries. International participants pay $60 USD. Contact us to find a time-zone-friendly batch. All sessions are 100% online via Zoom.

Will 16 sessions really be enough to get a job?

These 16 sessions give you the foundation and portfolio to start applying for junior roles and freelance work. Most career switchers continue learning independently after the camp — but the first job is achievable within 3–6 months of completing this.

What age is too old to learn coding?

There is no age limit. We have successfully taught learners in their 50s and 60s. The key is motivation — and you already have it. Modern tools and AI assistants make coding more accessible than ever before.

What if I miss a class?

All sessions are recorded. Watch anytime and book a 15-minute 1:1 catch-up with the instructor if needed. No one gets left behind.
