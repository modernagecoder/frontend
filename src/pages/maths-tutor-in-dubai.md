## A real worked example: percentages and the 5% VAT

A percent means "per hundred," so the UAE's 5% VAT is ×0.05 and a VAT-inclusive price is
×1.05:

- price before VAT AED 200 → ×1.05 = **AED 210**
- receipt shows AED 210 incl. VAT → 210 ÷ 1.05 = **AED 200** (undo the ×1.05)

No separate trick for adding versus removing VAT — one operation and its reverse. The same
idea handles tax, discounts, tips and interest.
