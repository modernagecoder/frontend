## FAQ
- **What projects can beginners build?** Word counters, image sliders, memory games and calculators first; then weather apps, typing challenges and interactive sites. First project usually within 2–3 months.
- **How long to build a complete project?** Simple: 1–2 weeks. Intermediate: 3–4 weeks. Advanced (AI chatbots, full-stack): 2–3 months.
- **Do students get certificates?** Yes — a completion certificate per major project, plus a feature here for the best.
- **What technologies do students learn?** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, Node.js, MongoDB, Firebase, AI/ML — stack depends on the course.
- **Can my child's project be featured?** Yes — any enrolled student can submit a completed project for review.
- **What age groups?** Kids (6–12), teens (13–18), college and professionals.
