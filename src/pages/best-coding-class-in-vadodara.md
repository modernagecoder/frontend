## Why Vadodara families start here

From Alkapuri and Sayajigunj to Fatehgunj and Akota, families across Vadodara are choosing live online coding classes that go beyond a certificate. We build for real skills — the kind that hold up in an interview or a first internship.

Vadodara has been teaching technology since 1890, when Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad III started the Kala Bhavan Technical Institute — an idea first floated at a prize-giving at Baroda College in 1886. It became MSU's Faculty of Technology and Engineering in 1949. The city stayed an engineering town: the district administration reckons over 35 per cent of India's power transmission and distribution equipment makers are here, with some 800 ancillaries around them. The route in is GUJCET and ACPC.

Whether your child is in a Gujarat GSEB, CBSE, ICSE or IGCSE school, the plan is the same: start at the right level, build something real, and keep the momentum going.
