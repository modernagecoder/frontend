## Why summer decides September

Teachers spend the first weeks of fall reteaching; math fades fastest over breaks because it is cumulative. But summer is also the one season a child can learn with a clear head, no test on Friday, which makes it the best window of the year to repair deep gaps properly. And the quiet prize is identity: eight weeks of patient wins sends back a child who raises their hand in week one.
