# Best online coding classes for kids in 2026, ranked honestly

**How we ranked:** 1) Depth per hour: a full live interactive taught hour, or gamified content? 2) Real building: do children ship actual projects with human feedback? 3) Value per taught hour.

## The ranking

1. **Modern Age Coders (our program, disclosed).** Live online school, coding + maths, ages 6 to 67. One full hour of deep, project-first teaching per class, twice a week, dedicated mentor; students ship real apps, games and AI tools (see /student-labs). Group $40/month, 1-on-1 $100 ($5 to $12.50 per class). 4.9 across 547 Google reviews. Free demo.
2. **Juni Learning.** The polished US option: American instructors, 1-on-1, from ~$275/month for 4 sessions (~$70/class).
3. **Scratch (free).** The world's best free start: MIT's block-coding platform, no teacher, perfect first taste.
4. **Outschool.** Marketplace variety at $10 to $30 per class hour; great sampling, you assemble the curriculum.
5. **Codingal.** Competition-centred live coding (India-based, global); plans ~Rs 8,999 to 12,999/month, ~Rs 800/class.
6. **Tynker.** Self-paced gamified app, ~$180/year; strongest for ages 5 to 7 before live classes make sense.

Sources: myelearningworld.com, brighterly.com, tynker.com, masandpas.com, grabon.in, scratch.mit.edu (July 2026).

## Decision shortcuts

Curious 5 to 7 year old → Scratch (free) or Tynker. Ready to really learn → Modern Age Coders (watch the free demo). US instructor required → Juni. Competition-driven → Codingal. Just exploring → Outschool. Coding + maths in one school → Modern Age Coders (the only one on the list).
