# Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## 1. Course Cancellation

You may cancel your enrollment in any of our online courses or live bootcamps at any time. However, cancellations made after the start of a live cohort or after accessing the digital materials may be subject to our [Refund Policy](/refund).

## 2. Subscription Cancellations

If you are subscribed to any recurring billing plan, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle, and you will not be charged again.

## 3. Cancellation by Modern Age Coders

We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule courses due to insufficient enrollments or unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, participants will be offered a full refund or the option to transfer to another schedule.

## 4. Impact of Cancellation

Upon cancellation, you will retain access to the platform and materials until the end of your current billing period (if applicable). Afterward, access to course materials and premium features will be suspended.

## 5. How to Cancel

To cancel a subscription or live course enrollment, please navigate to your account settings or contact our support team at [contact@modernagecoders.com](mailto:contact@modernagecoders.com).
