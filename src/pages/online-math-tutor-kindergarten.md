# Online math tutor for kindergarten: where liking math gets decided

**The map:** counting & cardinality (the song-vs-counting gap), comparing quantities with reasons, addition/subtraction as acted stories within 10, teen numbers as ten-and-more (the first place-value idea), shapes and positions, direct measurement comparison.

## The signature catch

The five-cookie moment: a child counts "1-2-3-4-5!" beautifully, then recounts when asked "so how many are there?" - the cardinality gap, invisible behind cheerful recitation, foundational to everything, fixed through bring-me-that-many games the child never experiences as remediation.
