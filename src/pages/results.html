The honest part

What we refuse to claim.

No invented numbers

• No "average grade improvement" percentages, we do not have audited data that would make such a number honest, and neither do the companies who publish them.

• No guaranteed scores or ranks. Exam results belong to students; our job is the preparation and the evidence trail along the way.

• No fabricated enrollment counts or fake urgency. The numbers on this page, 4.9, 547, are checkable at the source.

What we promise instead

• A free trial with an honest placement read, including "not yet" and "not us".

• Monthly billing with no lock-in, so we re-earn every family every four weeks, the strongest accountability structure we know.

• Evidence you can watch accumulate: recordings, projects, explain-backs, and for exam tracks, practice-score progressions.

The full promise is written on our guarantee page. It is short, and we mean it.

Fair questions

Questions skeptical parents ask about results.

Why should I trust a school that grades itself?

You should not, which is why the evidence on this page lives off our servers: reviews on Google's platform, projects with working public links, recordings you can watch before booking. Grade us with the same checklist you would use on anyone, we publish it on the free-trial page.

Where are your before-and-after score statistics?

Unpublished, because honest versions of those numbers require audited cohorts and controls nobody in this industry has. What we offer instead is per-family evidence: your own child's documented progression, visible month by month, judged by you under monthly billing that lets you leave whenever the evidence disappoints.

Are the Student Labs projects really built by students?

Yes, in classes, by named students, with mentors coaching rather than building. The projects are live links; open them, and note the rough edges that make them believably real. Polished perfection would be the tell.

How fast should we expect visible results?

Honest ranges: engagement changes within weeks (the child stops dreading, starts asking for class); maths homework comfort in 4-6 weeks; durable understanding and school-mark movement over a term; exam-track progressions across a prep cycle. Anyone promising faster is describing luck or lying.

What is your success rate?

Undefinable honestly, and we decline to invent it. What we can say: monthly billing means families who stay are choosing to stay, every month, with 547 of them having taken the time to write about it publicly.
