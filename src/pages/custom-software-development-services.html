Questions

Frequently asked

What is custom software development?

Custom software is built specifically for your business instead of bought off the shelf. It fits your exact workflow, integrates with your existing tools, and can grow with you. We design, build, test and maintain it end to end.

How long does a custom software project take?

A focused internal tool can take a few weeks, while a full platform can take a few months. After a free consultation we give a written timeline with milestones so you know what to expect.

Will I own the source code?

Yes. You receive the full source code, documentation and access on handover. There is no lock-in, and you can continue the work with any team.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. We regularly connect custom software to CRMs, ERPs, payment gateways, accounting tools and third party APIs so your data stays in one place.

What does it cost?

Each project is scoped to your requirement, so we quote after a free consultation. You get a written proposal with scope, timeline and a fixed or milestone based price before any work begins.
