# Learn from mentors, not videos.

Every class at Modern Age Coders is taught live by a person who knows your child's name, pace, and goals.

## Founder & Lead Mentor: Shivam Khemka

Known to hundreds of families simply as **Shivam Sir**.

Shivam founded Modern Age Coders in 2020 around one conviction: children do not need another app that teaches them to copy code. They need a mentor who teaches them to think. That is why every class here is live, every batch is small, and maths and code are taught together as one skill.

He still teaches every week. Ask the parents: in their Google reviews, families describe him and the mentor team as teachers, life counselors, and friends first.

> "The teaching approach of my legendary teachers, Shivam Sir, Mihir Sir and Sonu Sir, is extraordinary. They were not only my teachers, but also mentors, life counselors and most importantly, a friendly figure."
> — Vineeta Bhatter, mother of Krishnam, verified Google review

## How this team teaches

- **Live, always.** No recorded lectures pretending to be classes. A teacher is present, every time.
- **Small batches.** Group classes capped at 10 students, so nobody hides in the back row.
- **Projects from day one.** Students build real things early. See them live in our Student Labs.
- **Maths and code together.** One strengthens the other. It is the core of how we were built.
