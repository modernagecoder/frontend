SafeStep

An AI walking companion that gives sight back to the visually impaired.

SafeStep turns any ordinary smartphone into a pair of eyes. Worn facing forward, its camera reads the path ahead in real time and speaks calm, instant guidance — “step down ahead,” “person approaching on your left,” “clear for ten steps.” A double-tap fires an SOS that shares live location and a voice note with a chosen guardian. Best of all, it runs fully offline on low-cost Android phones — so independence never depends on the internet.

FarmSense

A pocket crop doctor that stops disease before it spreads.

FarmSense puts an agriculture expert in every farmer's pocket. Snap a photo of a sick leaf and, within seconds, it names the disease, explains it in the farmer's own language with a spoken message, and prescribes an exact, low-cost treatment using what's available locally. When several nearby farms report the same problem, FarmSense quietly warns the whole village of an outbreak — turning one farmer's photo into protection for an entire region.

StudyLoop

Turns any textbook page into a study plan that adapts to you.

StudyLoop turns a single photo of a textbook page into a complete study kit — a smart quiz, flashcards, and a 7-day revision plan — in under a minute. As you answer, it learns exactly which ideas you keep getting wrong and rebuilds the next round to drill those weak spots, so every minute of studying goes where it actually matters. It's like having a patient tutor who only teaches what you haven't mastered yet.
