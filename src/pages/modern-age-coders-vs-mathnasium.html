The honest part

Where each one genuinely wins

No provider is best for every family. Here is the fair version of the trade-off.

Where Mathnasium is a better fit

  • You want a physical place. Some children focus better out of the house, and a staffed center gives structure that home study cannot.
  • Drop-in flexibility. Members typically visit the center multiple times a week during open hours rather than fixed appointment slots.
  • In-person social pressure works for your child. Seeing other students working around them motivates some kids.
  • You specifically want the Mathnasium Method. Their diagnostic-and-worksheet system is a mature, US-wide franchise product.

Where Modern Age Coders wins

  • Real 1-on-1 teaching. Your child gets a dedicated mentor for the whole hour, not a floor instructor shared between tables.
  • Depth and application, not drilling. Our mentors teach the why behind the maths and apply it to real problems, in interactive classes children genuinely look forward to.
  • Coding and maths together. The same school can grow both skills in one integrated programme.
  • All of it for less than half the membership. $150 a month for true 1-on-1 versus a typical $300 to $450, with no enrollment fee and no commute.
  • Zero risk to try. A free live demo class with the actual teacher before any payment. Read our promise.

Honest answers

Frequently asked questions

Is Modern Age Coders cheaper than Mathnasium?

For most families, substantially. Mathnasium memberships typically run $300 to $450 a month in the US as of July 2026, plus a one-time enrollment fee parents report at $99 to $200. Modern Age Coders maths is $100 a month for small-group classes and $150 a month for true 1-on-1, which is 8 live 60-minute classes, with no enrollment fee and monthly billing you can stop anytime.

Is online 1-on-1 as effective as a tutoring center?

A dedicated live teacher who plans every session around one child is a different experience from a learning floor where instructors rotate between students. Online 1-on-1 gives your child the teacher's full attention for the whole hour. The honest caveat: some children genuinely focus better in a physical space, and if that is your child, a center may serve them better. Our free demo class is the quickest way to see which style fits.

Does Mathnasium teach coding?

No, Mathnasium is a maths-only programme. Modern Age Coders teaches coding and maths, separately or as one integrated track, so a child who is strong in one can grow the other with the same school and the same mentor relationship.

What does Modern Age Coders maths cost exactly?

For international students, small-group maths is $100 a month and 1-on-1 maths is $150 a month. Every plan is 2 live 60-minute classes a week, 8 a month, which works out to $12.50 to $18.75 per class. There are no registration fees and no contracts. Indian families see rupee pricing on our pricing page.

Can we try before paying?

Yes. Every student starts with a free live demo class with a real teacher, no card details required. You decide only after you have seen an actual lesson. That promise is written down on our zero-risk page.
