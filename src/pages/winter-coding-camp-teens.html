Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

No experience required! We start from the basics. If you've never written a line of code, you're in the right place. Prior exposure helps but isn't necessary.

What equipment do I need?

A laptop or desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux), stable internet connection, and headphones with microphone. All software we use is free.

How long is each session?

Each session is 90 minutes — long enough for meaningful learning but short enough to maintain focus. Every session includes hands-on coding practice.

I'm in the US/UK/UAE — can I join?

Absolutely! We have students from 30+ countries. International students pay $60 (USD). We schedule batches across time zones — contact us to find a batch that fits your schedule.

Can I use these skills for college or career?

Absolutely! HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python are the exact same skills used by professional developers. Your portfolio can be linked in college applications and CVs immediately.

What if I miss a class?

All sessions are recorded. You can watch the recording anytime and schedule a 15-minute doubt-clearing call with the instructor if needed.
