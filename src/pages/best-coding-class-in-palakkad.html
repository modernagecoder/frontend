2

An IIT on the Doorstep

When a national institute of technology sits at Kanjikode in your own district, "becoming an engineer" stops being a distant dream and becomes a visible, local path worth aiming at early.

From Watching to Making

The phone stops being a thing your child only consumes and becomes a thing they create on. That quiet flip — from audience to author — is one of the most useful habits a young person can pick up.

5

An Edge in KEAM, JEE & NEET

Kerala's entrance exams reward speed and structured thinking under pressure. Regular coding practice trains exactly that kind of reasoning — a quiet advantage in a district that takes entrances seriously.

6

A Portfolio, Not Just a Report Card

Real games, websites and apps become evidence a child can point to in a school science fair or a college interview — and in Palakkad, where everyone has good marks, evidence is what sets a student apart.

Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions from Palakkad parents about our coding classes. Can't find yours? Just message us.

Which is the best coding class in Palakkad for kids?

+

Modern Age Coders runs Palakkad's top-rated live online coding classes for kids aged 8–12. Children start with Scratch (games, animation, logic) and move to Python, in small batches of 5–8, taught by industry instructors. We serve all of Palakkad — Sultanpet, Stadium Bypass, Chandranagar, Kalmandapam, Olavakkode, Kanjikode, Koppam and Yakkara, plus Ottapalam, Shoranur and Mannarkkad.

Are the classes online or offline, and do you cover all of Palakkad town and the outskirts?

+

All classes are live and online, so we reach every corner of the district the same way — Sultanpet and the Fort Maidan in the centre, the Olavakkode railway side, Chandranagar and Vadakkanthara, the Kanjikode industrial-and-IIT belt, and out to Ottapalam, Shoranur, Pattambi and Mannarkkad. Your child joins the same expert teacher from home — no autorickshaw ride into town. A live session here means the teacher and students see each other's screens in real time, code together, and every class is recorded for later.

How much do coding classes cost in Palakkad?

+

Group classes (5–8 students) start at ₹1,499/month for 2 live classes a week. Mini Batch (3–4 students) is ₹2,499/month, and personalised 1-on-1 mentoring is ₹4,999/month. Every new student gets a free demo class first, and there are no hidden charges.

Will coding help with KEAM, JEE, NEET and the Kerala/CBSE board exams?

+

Yes. Palakkad takes entrances seriously, and with IIT Palakkad nearby the bar is high. Coding strengthens the exact logical reasoning, decomposition and problem-solving that KEAM, JEE and Olympiads reward, and it directly supports CBSE/Kerala-board Computer Science and the new NEP-2020 AI modules. Our Python and Java tracks line up with what's taught at Bhavan's Vidya Mandir, Kendriya Vidyalaya, Chinmaya and Govt Moyan.

What's the best age to start coding?

+

Age 8 is ideal to begin with visual, block-based coding (Scratch). For text-based languages like Python and Java, 12–13 onwards works best. We run separate tracks for kids (8–12), teens (13–18) and college/working learners (18+), so the starting point always matches the child.

Do you prepare Palakkad college students for placements?

+

Yes — our college track is placement-focused: 200+ DSA problems, mock interviews, a MERN full-stack build and an AI/Data-Science portfolio. It's designed for students from IIT Palakkad, NSS College of Engineering, Government Victoria College and the wider Palakkad–Coimbatore engineering corridor targeting product companies and the Kochi/Coimbatore IT belts.

What equipment does my child need?

+

Just a laptop or desktop and a stable internet connection — both increasingly common in Palakkad homes. A computer (rather than a tablet) is best for hands-on practice. Minimum: Windows 10 / macOS, 4GB RAM, broadband. All the software we use is free, and our team helps with setup before the first class.

What are the class timings? Do you have weekend batches?

+

Yes. Weekday batches run 4 PM–8 PM (after school) and weekend batches 9 AM–6 PM (Sat & Sun). Palakkad children juggle school, tuitions and arts/sports, so we work around your schedule. Classes are an hour long, twice a week.

What if my child doesn't enjoy it — is there a refund?

+

The free demo lets you experience our teaching before committing. If after the first paid week your child isn't satisfied, we offer a full refund — no questions asked. We would rather have a child who looks forward to class than a parent tied to a plan they regret.

Do you run summer & winter coding camps in Palakkad?

+

Yes — intensive camps run during the summer break (May–June) and winter break (Dec–Jan), where a child can finish a full course in 4–6 weeks. They're a favourite among Palakkad parents who want a productive, screen-positive holiday. Ask us for upcoming dates and early-bird offers.
