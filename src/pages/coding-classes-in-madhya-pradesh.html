Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best online coding classes in Madhya Pradesh?+

Modern Age Coders offers live online coding classes in Madhya Pradesh for kids aged 6–12, teens aged 13–18 and adults. Courses include Scratch, Python, Web Development, Java, AI/ML and Data Structures & Algorithms. Classes run in small batches of 5–8 students with expert instructors, and students across Indore, Bhopal and Jabalpur can join. Every new learner gets a free demo class.

Do you offer coding classes for MP Board, CBSE and ICSE students?+

Yes. Our coding and maths curriculum supports students from the MP Board (MPBSE), CBSE and ICSE. Lessons are designed to complement school work and strengthen logical thinking and problem solving alongside the school syllabus.

How much do coding classes cost in Madhya Pradesh?+

Group coding classes start at ₹1,499 per month for 2 classes a week. A Mini Batch of 3–4 students is ₹2,499 per month, and personalised 1-on-1 classes are ₹4,999 per month. A free demo class is available before you enrol.

Are classes available in Hindi as well as English?+

Yes. Our instructors teach in English and can explain concepts in Hindi when a student is more comfortable, so children across Madhya Pradesh can learn coding in the language that suits them best.

Which cities in Madhya Pradesh do you serve?+

Because classes are live and online, we serve every district of Madhya Pradesh. We have dedicated city pages for Indore, Bhopal and Jabalpur, and we also teach students in Gwalior, Ujjain, Sagar, Rewa, Satna, Ratlam and beyond.

Can your maths classes help with JEE preparation?+

Yes. Our advanced maths programme builds the strong fundamentals needed for JEE — useful alongside Indore's strong engineering-prep culture and IIT Indore. We focus on concept clarity, problem-solving speed and reasoning rather than rote learning.

What age can a child start learning to code?+

Children can start as early as age 6 with visual block coding in Scratch, which teaches logic through games and animation. From around age 10 students move into Python, and teenagers progress to web development, Java and AI projects.

Are the classes live or recorded?+

All classes are live and interactive with a real instructor, so students can ask questions in real time. Each session is also recorded and shared, so learners in Madhya Pradesh can revise lessons anytime.
