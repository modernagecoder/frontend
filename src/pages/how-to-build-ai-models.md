The professional process, broken into skills you can repeat for any project.

### Frame & Data

Turn a messy real-world goal into a clear prediction task, then collect, clean and engineer the data your model will learn from.

### Build & Train

Choose the right algorithm for the problem and train it in Python — understanding why, not just which function to call.

### Evaluate & Improve

Measure performance honestly, spot overfitting, tune the model, and iterate until it genuinely works on new data.
