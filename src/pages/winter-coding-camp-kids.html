Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need any prior coding experience?

Not at all! We start completely from scratch (pun intended). The first session assumes zero experience. We have had kids as young as 5 thrive in our program.

What equipment does my child need?

A laptop or desktop (Windows, Mac, or Linux), a stable internet connection, and headphones with a microphone. All software is free — just a web browser required for Scratch.

How long is each session?

60 to 75 minutes per session — long enough for meaningful learning, short enough to keep kids engaged. Sessions are interactive with breaks built in.

My child is 5. Is that too young?

We have successfully taught children as young as 5. Contact us so we can assess your child's readiness and match them to the right batch and mentor.

Will my child be safe online?

All sessions are password-protected Zoom classrooms with trusted educators. We follow strict child-safety policies and all instructors are background-verified.

What if my child misses a class?

All sessions are recorded. Your child can watch the recording anytime and schedule a 15-minute catch-up with the instructor if needed.
