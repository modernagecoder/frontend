## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our college courses

### Which course should I start with for placements?

Start with DSA if you're preparing for product companies. For service companies, Full Stack or Python is a great choice. We recommend DSA + one specialization for best results.

### How long does it take to become job-ready?

With dedicated effort, 4-6 months for a single specialization. For comprehensive placement prep (DSA + Full Stack), plan for 8-12 months of consistent learning.

### Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We provide resume building, mock interviews, portfolio reviews, and guidance on applying to companies. Many of our students have secured positions at top tech companies.

### Can I balance courses with college studies?

Absolutely! Our courses are self-paced with flexible scheduling. Most students dedicate 2-3 hours daily or more on weekends. Weekend batches are also available.

### Are the certificates recognized by companies?

Our certificates are recognized and valued by employers. However, what matters most is the skills and projects you build, and that's what our courses focus on.

### Which coding language should college students learn first?

Python is the best starting point for most college students. It is beginner-friendly, widely used in data science, AI, and web development, and is the top language asked in campus placements. If your goal is web development, start with JavaScript. For competitive programming, C++ is the go-to choice.

### How do coding courses help with campus placements?

Campus placement tests at most companies include coding rounds, DSA problems, and system design questions. Our courses teach you exactly what recruiters test for. You build portfolio projects on GitHub, practice mock interviews, and learn to solve problems under time pressure. Students who complete our DSA + Full Stack track consistently clear placement rounds at top companies.

### Can I learn coding alongside my college degree?

Yes, all our courses are designed for students who are studying full-time. Classes are flexible with evening, weekend, and custom scheduling options. Most students dedicate 1 to 2 hours daily and make strong progress within a few months. Our live 1-on-1 format means you never miss a session because every class is scheduled at your convenience.
