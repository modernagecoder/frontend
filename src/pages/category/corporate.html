Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our corporate training programs

What is the minimum team size for corporate training?
We offer corporate training for teams of 5 or more employees. For smaller teams, we can discuss customized solutions or group them with other organizations for cost-effective training.
Can training be conducted at our office?
Yes! We offer both online and on-site training options. Our instructors can come to your office, or we can conduct sessions virtually via Zoom/Teams based on your preference and convenience.
How long are the training programs?
Program duration varies based on the course and depth required. Typically, programs range from 2-week intensive bootcamps to 3-month comprehensive courses. We customize the duration based on your team's needs and availability.
Do you provide certificates?
Yes, all participants receive completion certificates. We also provide detailed progress reports and skill assessments for HR and management review to track team development and ROI.
What industries do you serve?
We serve all industries including IT, Finance, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Retail, Education, and more. Our curriculum is customized to address industry-specific challenges and use cases.
What is the pricing structure?
Pricing depends on the training program, duration, team size, and delivery mode (online/on-site). Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements. We offer competitive rates with flexible payment options.
