Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about teen coding courses

Which course should my teen start with?

For beginners, we recommend Python Programming or Frontend Coding. If your teen has some experience, they can start with Java, React, or Full Stack Development based on their interests.

How long does each course take?

Most courses are 3-6 months long with 2-3 sessions per week. We offer flexible scheduling including weekend batches to accommodate school schedules.

Will this help with college applications?

Absolutely! Students build real projects that can be showcased in portfolios. Coding skills and certificates strengthen college applications, especially for CS and engineering programs.

Are the courses suitable for competitive exams?

Yes! Our Problem Solving & DSA course is specifically designed to help with JEE, competitive programming, and coding interviews. Many students have cracked top exams after our courses.

Can teens get internships after completing courses?

Many of our students have secured internships at startups and tech companies. We help students build portfolios and provide guidance on applying for opportunities.
