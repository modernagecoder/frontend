Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our college courses

Which course should I start with for placements?

Start with DSA if you're preparing for product companies. For service companies, Full Stack or Python is a great choice. We recommend DSA + one specialization for best results.

How long does it take to become job-ready?

With dedicated effort, 4-6 months for a single specialization. For comprehensive placement prep (DSA + Full Stack), plan for 8-12 months of consistent learning.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We provide resume building, mock interviews, portfolio reviews, and guidance on applying to companies. Many of our students have secured positions at top tech companies.

Can I balance courses with college studies?

Absolutely! Our courses are self-paced with flexible scheduling. Most students dedicate 2-3 hours daily or more on weekends. Weekend batches are also available.

Are the certificates recognized by companies?

Our certificates are recognized and valued by employers. However, what matters most is the skills and projects you build — and that's what our courses focus on.
