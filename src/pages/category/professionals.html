Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from working professionals

Can I learn while working full-time?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed for busy professionals. We offer weekend batches, evening sessions, and self-paced options. Most students dedicate 8-10 hours per week.

How long does it take to switch careers?

For a complete career switch, plan for 6-9 months of dedicated learning. This includes mastering fundamentals, building projects, and preparing for interviews.

Do you offer corporate training?

Yes! We provide customized corporate training programs for teams of all sizes. Includes tailored curriculum, flexible scheduling, progress tracking, and dedicated support.

Is age a barrier for learning to code?

Not at all! We have successful students from 25 to 55+ years old. Your professional experience is actually an advantage — it brings domain knowledge and problem-solving skills.

What support do you provide after the course?

You get lifetime access to course materials, alumni community access, career guidance, resume reviews, and mock interview sessions. We're invested in your long-term success.
