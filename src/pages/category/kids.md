## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents want to know about kids coding

### What age is best to start coding?

Kids can start as early as 6 years old with visual block coding. Our courses are designed for ages 6-12, with age-appropriate content and teaching methods.

### How much time should kids spend coding?

We recommend 30-60 minutes per session, 2-3 times per week. This keeps kids engaged without overwhelming them, allowing time for other activities.

### Do kids need any prior experience?

No prior experience needed! Our courses start from absolute basics. Kids just need curiosity and willingness to learn and experiment.

### What skills will my child develop?

Beyond coding, kids develop logical thinking, problem-solving, creativity, patience, and confidence. These skills benefit them in all subjects and life situations.

### Are certificates provided?

Yes! Kids receive certificates upon course completion. These can be added to school portfolios and boost their confidence in their achievements.
