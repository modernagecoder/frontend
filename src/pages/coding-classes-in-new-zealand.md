## NCEA grading explained
- **Excellence (E)** — Top tier; the bar for serious CS aspirants
- **Merit (M)** — Strong; comfortable competence
- **Achieved (A)** — Pass; earns credits but doesn't differentiate
- **Not Achieved (N)** — No credit
For University Entrance: 14+ credits at L3 in three approved subjects + literacy + numeracy thresholds. **Excellence-graded L3 DT credits** are the differentiator.
