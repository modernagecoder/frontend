Why BHS Mukundapur Students Need a Coding & Maths Edge

South-east Kolkata's most trusted new CBSE school carries 83 years of Birla legacy. We make sure your child's skills match that legacy.

83 Years of Birla Legacy — Now in Your Neighbourhood

Opened on 20 April 2018, Birla High School Mukundapur is the Vidya Mandir Society's deliberate expansion into south-east Kolkata — bringing the same "Tamaso Ma Jyotirgamay" (Lead From Darkness to Light) philosophy and CBSE rigour of the original 1941 Moira Street campus to families in Kalikapur, Santoshpur, Patuli, and beyond. This is also the Birla group's first co-educational school — boys and girls learning together under the same trusted banner. With a 6:1 student-teacher ratio (far better than the Kolkata average), AC smart classrooms, WiFi campus, GPS buses, and foreign languages from KG, BHS Mukundapur offers a premium education without the city-centre commute. We are not affiliated with BHS Mukundapur — but we are their students' most aligned after-school coding and maths partner.

Informatics Practices & Data Science — The Subjects Most Coaches Miss

Unlike the original BHS Moira Street (which offers Computer Science), BHS Mukundapur's Class 11–12 senior electives include Informatics Practices (IP) and Data Science (DS) — two CBSE subjects that most coaching centres in Kolkata simply don't know how to teach. IP covers Python with real-world libraries (pandas, numpy, matplotlib), SQL, database connectivity, and data visualisation. DS goes deeper into statistical analysis, machine learning concepts, and applied data projects. These aren't theory subjects — the practical exam component is substantial, and students who can't write working code from scratch struggle. Our dedicated IP & DS Bootcamp is built around exactly this gap, covering every practical requirement for the CBSE board exam while building genuinely useful data skills for life beyond school.

The Medical Hub Advantage — And What It Means for Your Child

Mukundapur is now Kolkata's premier medical corridor — AMRI Hospital, Medica Superspecialty, Apollo Gleneagles, Rabindranath Tagore International Institute of Cardiac Sciences (Narayana Health), and Sankara Nethralaya all within walking distance of BHS Mukundapur. This means a large portion of BHS Mukundapur's parent community are doctors, surgeons, hospital administrators, and healthcare executives — highly educated, data-aware, globally-minded professionals who understand the value of analytical skills. These parents already know that coding and data literacy are as important as biology for the next generation. They choose BHS for the legacy; they choose Modern Age Coders to complete the picture. We understand this community — and we pitch our programme accordingly: rigorous, results-oriented, and genuinely useful.

Flexible Batches — Orange Line Metro, No Commute Needed

South-east Kolkata used to mean "far from everything good." Not anymore. The Orange Line metro — with Satyajit Ray station just 1.71 km from BHS Mukundapur — now connects the area to the rest of the city. Our classes, however, are entirely live online. No commute from Kalikapur, Santoshpur, Patuli, Survey Park, Panchasayar, or the luxury towers at Utalika and Avidipta. Weekday evening batches run from 4 PM to 8 PM. Full weekend batches run Saturday and Sunday, 9 AM to 7 PM. Every single session is recorded — miss a class for a science fair, sports day, or Durga Puja? The recording is waiting. This flexibility is why families across south-east Kolkata — and even from Jadavpur and Garia — choose us as their trusted coding and maths partner.

Questions from BHS Mukundapur Parents

Everything you need to know before booking your first class.

Do you offer classes specifically for BHS Mukundapur students?+
We are not affiliated with Birla High School Mukundapur, but our classes are extremely popular among BHS Mukundapur students from Kalikapur, Santoshpur, Patuli, and south-east Kolkata. Our curriculum aligns precisely with the CBSE board BHS Mukundapur follows — including Computer Applications (Class 9–10), Informatics Practices and Data Science (Class 11–12), and CBSE Maths across all classes.
BHS Mukundapur offers IP and Data Science — do you cover those?+
Yes — and this is what makes us uniquely relevant for BHS Mukundapur. Informatics Practices and Data Science are BHS Mukundapur's senior elective tracks at Class 11–12. We cover the full curriculum: Python with pandas, numpy, matplotlib and seaborn, SQL, database connectivity, data analysis projects, and all CBSE practical exam requirements. Most general coaching centres in Kolkata don't know how to teach these subjects — we do.
What batches are available near BHS Mukundapur in Kalikapur?+
All classes are live online — no commute from Mukundapur, Kalikapur, Santoshpur, Patuli, Survey Park, or anywhere in south-east Kolkata. Weekday evening batches run 4 PM – 8 PM. Full weekend batches run Saturday & Sunday, 9 AM – 7 PM. Every session is recorded, so students never miss content due to school activities, sports day, or Durga Puja. The new Orange Line metro (Satyajit Ray station, 1.71 km from BHS) also makes any rare in-person workshop easily accessible.
Is this programme suitable for girls at BHS Mukundapur (co-ed school)?+
Absolutely — and this is an important point. BHS Mukundapur is the Birla group's first co-educational school, unlike the original BHS Moira Street which is boys-only. Our classes have always been co-ed and we consistently see girls outperform in structured coding and data science environments. We also offer a dedicated Coding for Girls programme if that's of interest. Both boys and girls from BHS Mukundapur are equally welcome and equally served.
At what age can my child start coding?+
BHS Mukundapur students can start from Playgroup/Nursery age (4–6) with introductory computational thinking activities, and formally from Class 1 with our Scratch block-coding programme. Classes 4–5 move to HTML/CSS. From Class 6, Python is the core language. AI/ML tracks begin from Class 8. Informatics Practices and Data Science bootcamps are for Class 11–12. We'll recommend the best entry point in your free demo class.
How much do classes cost?+
Group classes (4–6 students) start at ₹1499/month with 2 live sessions per week. Mini Batch (3-4 students) at ₹2499/month or 1:1 personalised classes at ₹4999/month. The IP & Data Science Bootcamp is ₹1999/month. All plans include a free demo class with zero payment or commitment required.
Do you offer JEE Maths preparation for BHS Mukundapur students?+
Yes — JEE Foundation from Class 8 onwards and full JEE Main + Advanced Maths for Class 11–12. Science stream students at BHS Mukundapur targeting IITs and NITs benefit enormously from starting early. We cover algebra, calculus, coordinate geometry, probability, and vectors well beyond the standard CBSE school syllabus — with the problem-solving depth that competitive entrance exams demand.
Do you offer summer coding camps for BHS Mukundapur students?+
Yes! Intensive summer camps run in April–May and Durga Puja holiday camps in October. Students complete a full course module in 3–4 accelerated weeks. Themes include Python & AI, Web App Development, Data Science, and Competitive Programming. Contact us for 2026 summer camp dates and early-bird pricing.
Can I see how you teach before paying for classes?+
Yes — and we encourage it. Visit our free How We Teach demo library to watch real, full-length class recordings for both coding and maths, organised by topic and age. There is no sign-up and no payment, so you can see our exact teaching style before booking your child's free demo class.
