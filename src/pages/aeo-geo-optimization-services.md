Your customers are asking ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and Google AI before they ever reach a search page. We make your brand the source those engines quote and recommend.

## AEO vs GEO vs SEO

- **SEO** ranks the link on a results page. Still essential, but a shrinking share of attention.
- **AEO** wins the answer, so Google AI Overviews, featured snippets and voice assistants read your words.
- **GEO** gets you cited, so ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity name and recommend your brand.

You need all three. They share technical foundations, but the content, structure and authority signals differ.
