Honest answers

Frequently asked questions

What is the main difference between Modern Age Coders and Codingal?

Philosophy. Codingal organises learning around coding competitions, contests and certificates, which suits kids who are motivated by winning. Modern Age Coders organises learning around depth: a mentor teaching the why for a full interactive hour, real projects that ship, and maths taught alongside the code because the two skills compound. Certificates follow from real ability here, not the other way around.

How do the prices compare?

Codingal's published plans run around Rs 8,999 to Rs 12,999 a month as of July 2026, with per-class listings near Rs 800 and larger packs from about Rs 29,500. Modern Age Coders group classes are Rs 1,499 a month and true 1-on-1 is Rs 4,999 a month, both with 8 live one-hour classes and no packs to buy. Depth does not require the bigger number.

Does Codingal teach maths?

Codingal is a coding-first company. Modern Age Coders teaches coding and maths as first-class subjects in one school, which matters because the maths is exactly what turns a copy-paste coder into a real problem solver.

My child loves competitions. Should we pick Codingal?

Maybe, and that is an honest answer. If contests are the main motivation, their model is built for it. Our students compete too, but as a byproduct of depth rather than the goal. If you want the competition itch scratched AND real engineering ability, take a free demo with us first and see whether depth hooks your child harder than a leaderboard.

Can we try Modern Age Coders before paying?

Yes. Every student starts with a free live demo class with a real mentor, no card details required, and billing is monthly with no lock-in. Details on our zero-risk page.
