## A real worked example: what a percentage really is

A percent is a fraction with a fixed bottom of 100 — "per hundred." One idea behind every
percentage question:

- 18% = 18/100 = 0.18
- 18% of a $1,250 bill: 0.18 × 1250 = **$225**
- price rose $80 → $100: change ÷ original = 20/80 = **25% up**

No separate trick for "of," "increase" or "discount" — tips, taxes, interest, markups and
budgets are all the same move. This is the foundation we build statistics and financial math on.
