## 4. Tue PM — Maths trick
**Caption:**
Quick one. To multiply any two digit number by 11, split the digits and add them in the middle.

Take 52 times 11. Split the 5 and the 2, add them to get 7, drop it in the middle. 572. That is it.

Maths is full of these once someone shows you how it actually works instead of making you memorise. We teach maths like this, properly. Free demo in bio.
