## üéØ Pro Tips for Better Results

### 1. **Color Consistency**
Always include: "purple (#8A2BE2) and teal (#4ECDC4) accent colors" in your prompts

### 2. **Avoid These Common Issues**
- ‚ùå "Text in image" (AI struggles with readable text)
- ‚ùå "Specific brand logos" (copyright issues)
- ‚ùå "Too many elements" (keeps it clean)
- ‚úÖ Use symbols and icons instead of text
- ‚úÖ Focus on 2-3 main elements
- ‚úÖ Keep composition simple and clear

### 3. **Iteration Strategy**
1. Generate 4 variations
2. Pick the best one
3. Use "remix" or "vary" to refine
4. Upscale to highest quality
5. Download and optimize

### 4. **Negative Prompts** (What to Avoid)
Add to your prompts:
```
Negative prompt: blurry, low quality, distorted faces, text, watermark, cluttered, messy, dark, gloomy, unprofessional
```
